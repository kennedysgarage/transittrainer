Transit Trainer

So you've made it to New York City and if adjusting to the buzz and glitz of the city wasn't enough, you've got to now navigate the subway transit system. Transit Trainer is a new puzzle app that helps you learn the train system. Its a fun, easy knowledge building app, guaranteed to make you a more confident straphanger. Move up in levels as you answer train line specific questions identifying subway stops, until you master the subway like a native New Yorker.
